# Privacy Policy

**Fitness Exporter** — Last updated: 6 March 2026

## What data the app accesses

Fitness Exporter reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:

- Daily step count
- Daily flights climbed

No other health or personal data is accessed.

## How your data is used

Your health data is used solely to export it in the format you choose. The app supports two export modes:

- **HTTP mode**: Your data is sent as JSON to a server URL that you configure. The app does not control or operate this server — you are responsible for choosing a trusted destination.
- **File mode**: Your data is saved as JSON files to the app's local Documents folder on your device. No data leaves your device in this mode.

## Data collection

The developer does not collect, store, or have access to any of your health data. There is no developer-operated server, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

## Data sharing and tracking

Your data is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or used to track you across apps or websites.

## Authentication credentials

If you configure a bearer token for HTTP export, it is stored in the iOS Keychain (encrypted, hardware-protected). It is never logged or transmitted to anyone other than the server you configure.
